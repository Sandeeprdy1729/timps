## Responsibilities

- Review PRs within your area within 48 hours
- Respond to issues within 24 hours
- Participate in monthly maintainer meetings
- Ensure CI/CD passes
- Release management (for your area)

## Becoming a Maintainer

To become a maintainer:

1. Have 10+ substantial PRs merged
2. Demonstrated knowledge of codebase
3. Active participation (6+ months)
4. Nomination by existing maintainer
5. BDFL approval

## Stepping Down

Maintainers can step down by:

1. Opening an issue
2. Transitioning responsibilities
3. Moving to emeritus status

## Emeritus Status

Former maintainers can be granted emeritus status:

- Still listed in MAINTAINERS.md
- No active responsibilities
- Can be recalled if needed
